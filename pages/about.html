My Engineering Manifesto

Hello, I'm Promise

My journey with technology began in childhood, with an insatiable curiosity about how things worked. While others saw broken electronics as trash, I saw puzzles waiting to be solved. This innate drive to understand and fix systems grew into a lifelong passion for computing and creative problem solving.

My personal projects showcase this evolution, from building simple tools like calculators, contact forms, blog apps and todo lists, to creating interactive portfolio websites, and even exploring network security concepts. Each project represented a new challenge that pushed my boundaries and deepened my understanding of technology's potential.

My passion reached even new heights during my internship at Skye8, where I had the privilege to work on divers problems and real-world challenges. The experience not only sharpened my technical skills but crystallized my vision for creating meaningful technological solutions. I discovered the thrill of seeing my work impact real users while collaborating with talented professionals who shared my enthusiasm for innovation.

What excites me most is the endless learning potential in technology. Every system I fix, every application I build, and every problem I solve adds to my growing toolkit. I'm driven by the belief that with the right combination of curiosity, persistence, and creativity, any technical challenge can be transformed into an opportunity.
